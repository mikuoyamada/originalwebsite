Adelaide

Adelaide is the capital city of South Australia, and is the fifth largest capital city in Australia. Consistently ranked as one of the top ten most liveable cities in the world, Adelaide is a popular destination for international students in Australia. This is because the city offers a balanced lifestyle with world-class education institutions and thriving local economy.
